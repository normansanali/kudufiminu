    Melissa Gisoni Facts

  • In 2012, Melissa and her daughters worked with Starlight Children’s Foundation which works to support chronically ill youth and raise awareness about fatal diseases and syndromes that afflict young children.
  • She was featured in the music video of Todrick Hall‘s 2014 song titled Freaks Like Me.
  • Melissa made a cameo appearance in a 2012 episode of the popular TV series The 47th Floor, portraying the character of Skylar Nae. She also participated, with her daughters, in a 2019 episode of the long-running and iconic game show Celebrity Family Feud.
  • Melissa has often been compared to American actress and comedian Amy Poehler due to the striking facial resemblance between the two.
  • The show Dance Moms was pitched to Melissa directly by one of its producers, John Corella, who was traveling across the United States to cast mothers of young dancers. The show was initially supposed to be called Just Dance.
  • After leaving Dance Moms in 2016, Melissa stated in an interview that she had never wanted to be on TV and would never do it again in the future. She had just hoped for her daughters to get a platform to showcase their talent but was very upset when people attacked/bullied her family online or made fake accounts of her daughters.
  • Melissa works as the final authority on her daughter Maddie’s work projects. Whatever offers Maddie’s agent and manager come up with has to get approved only through Melissa who also attends casting meetings.
  • Melissa had filed for bankruptcy in 2009 owing to mortgage debts from 3 properties and unsecured nonpriority claims. The case was discharged in 2010.
