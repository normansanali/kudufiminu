Who Is Kaya Evdokia? Meet Julian Casablanca Wife: Relationship And Musical Journey

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Julian Casablancas is well known as the lead vocalist of the classic rock band The Strokes. He has had an interesting personal journey. He married Juliet Joslin, The Strokes’ assistant manager at the time, in 2005. In more recent events, Julian married Kaya Evdokia for the second time in 2021, after a 15-year marriage with Joslin.

Juliet Joslin’s Journey

In 2002, Julian Casablancas and Juliet Joslin met. Three years after meeting, the couple decided to marry to cement their bond. Their relationship resulted in the birth of two boys, Cal and Zephyr Casablancas. However, love tales aren’t always easy; they had difficulties, which led to their separation in 2015 and final divorce in 2019.

A New Chapter Featuring Kaya Evdokia

Julian was dating Kaya Evdokia in 2020, whom he met while working as a roadie for The Strokes. Their love story advanced swiftly, ending in a secret wedding ceremony in 2021. Unlike Julian’s previous marriage, this one has remained out of the public glare, and the pair has yet to have children.

Julian’s Musical Journey

Julian’s impact on the music business is evident, even outside of his personal life. He has often voiced his dissatisfaction with the direction of popular music. He is now preparing for a summer concert tour and demonstrating his flexibility by playing with a second band, The Voidz.

Relationships and the Public Eye

Julian’s relationships, particularly with his wives, have been the subject of both public scrutiny and praise. His marriage to Joslin was well publicized, although his relationship with Evdokia was kept private.

Importance in Julian’s Life

Julian’s life and profession have clearly been influenced by his relationships. Both of his marriages, albeit diverse in their own ways, were crucial in molding him.

Embracing Change

Julian Casablancas personifies the adage “change is the only constant.” Julian’s own life story is a testimony to accepting change, from his marriage with Joslin to finding love again with Evdokia.
