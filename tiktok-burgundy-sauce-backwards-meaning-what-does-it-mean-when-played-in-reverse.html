TikTok: Burgundy Sauce Backwards Meaning- What Does It Mean When Played In Reverse?

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Due to the insulting nature of the so-called ‘burgundy sauce backward’ challenge, TikTok users are now attempting to ‘delete’ it.

Users are challenged to video themselves uttering “burgundy sauce” and then play it backward using Snapchat’s features.

On Instagram, the challenge has roughly 27 million views, with many users now using the hashtag #burgundysauce to warn others not to undertake it.

Here are more of the details that you need to know.

What is the TikTok ‘burgundy sauce backward’ challenge about?

When the phrase “burgundy sauce” is recorded and reversed, an offensive racial slur – “saucy n****r*” – is audible.

TikTok fans claim they’re fascinated about the challenge and plan to try it, but they refuse to broadcast the backward recording after being surprised. An example of this can be found below, courtesy of TikTok user @andrewiannazzo.

Burgundy Sauce: What Does It Mean When Played In Reverse?

When people reverse Burgundy Sauce, the effects aren’t always favorable.

If you’re willing to hear the saucy portion of the phrase, you might not like the reverse.

According to several reports, people hear racist slurs and other concerns that offend many individuals, groups, and communities. And this isn’t something to giggle about.

According to Urban Dictionary, pronouncing burgundy sauce backward is a racist insult. Unfortunately, many others have been coached to say the term to deceive them into thinking they are racist.

The users are also advocating using the phrase “vinegar,” which when reversed sounds like a racial insult.

The TikTok users are urging the community to cancel the music to avoid using the song “Burgundy Sauce.” In addition, they want to urge all the tiktokers to stop making the “Burgundy Sauce Backwards” a trend to avoid using the song.

In addition, the TikTokers are urging not to participate in the “meme” on social media and avoid interacting regarding it.
