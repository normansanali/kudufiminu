Marion Richard Obituary, Death Cause – On Saturday, my Godmother and my Dad’s younger sister, my Aunt Marion, passed away at the age of 67. Her death was rather sudden and unexpected. She was my Godmother at the time. My entire family is in shock and can’t believe what they’re hearing. In addition to her stunning good looks and enchanting voice, Aunt Marion possesses a generous and kind spirit. My children gave her the nickname “the dancing girl” since she was always up for having a good time on the dance floor at our family occasions, including weddings.

This included both my children and I. She was an exceptional athlete in addition to being a retired elementary school teacher, and she used her participation in events such as the New York City Marathon to raise money for the Sandy Hook Promise organization. She also taught elementary school. Even at that moment, she had already begun her training for the Iron Man triathlon that she will participate in later on in the fall. In recognition of her dedication and in remembering of the pursuits that brought her the utmost happiness, I plan to utilize the hashtag “Run4Marion” over the course of the upcoming weekend.

I have no doubt that this would bring her a great deal of joy. Could you kindly think of my Aunt and dedicate a section of your next race (or bike ride, or swim, etc.) to her in her honor so that you can celebrate her life?
