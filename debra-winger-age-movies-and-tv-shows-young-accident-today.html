Debra Winger is an American actress and producer. Winger was a co-executive producer on the Academy Award-nominated documentary Gasland in 2010.

She also served as the executive producer of the 2012 documentary Bel Borba Aqui, which chronicled the life and works of Brazilian graphic artist Bel Borba.

Debra Winger Age

How old is Debra Winger? Debra Winger was born on May 16, 1955. She is 67 years old.

Debra Winger Movies and Tv Shows

What movies and tv shows have Debra Winger been in? Debra Winger’s film roles include Urban Cowboy (1980), Legal Eagles (1986), Black Widow (1987), Betrayed (1988), The Sheltering Sky (1990), Forget Paris (1995), and Rachel Getting Married (2008).

Debra Winger Accident

Was Debra Winger in an accident? Debra Winger suffered a cerebral hemorrhage in a car accident at the age of 18 and was blind and partially paralyzed for ten months.

She was told that she would never be able to see again but recovered and moved to California to start acting.

Debra Winger Today

What is Debra Winger doing now? Debra Winger currently plays Ruth, the mother of an LA teacher in “Mr. Corman” on Apple TV+
