Mary Peltola Age, Biography

Mary Peltola was born on August 31, 1973, with the given name Mary Sattler in Anchorage, Alaska, USA. She is of American nationality and of American-White ethnicity. She grew up in the towns of Kwethluk, Tuntutuliak, Platinum, and Bethel. In 2023, she will celebrate her 50th birthday. Ward H. Sattler, her father, was a pilot and businessman who ran unsuccessfully for the Alaska House of Representatives in 2004, 2006, and 2008. She has ten brothers and sisters.

In terms of education, she worked as a herring and salmon technician for the Alaska Department of Fish and Game as a college student. She studied elementary education at the University of Northern Colorado from 1991 to 1994, then at the University of Alaska Fairbanks from 1994 to 1995, the University of Alaska Southeast from 1995 to 1997, and the University of Alaska Anchorage from 1997 to 1998.
