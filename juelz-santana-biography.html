Who is Juelz Santana?

Juelz Santana is a popular American rapper and actor.

He is best known for being the youngest member of the east coast hip-hop group, The Diplomats.

Juelz Santana: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

His birth name is Laron Louis James and was born on February 18, 1982, in New York City, United States. His mother’s name is Deborah James and is an African-American.

Juelz’s father is Dominican. His father’s name is unknown. He has raised in the Harlem section of Manhattan. Juelz has four brothers in his family namely: DeMar James, Malik James, Mark James, and Jermaine James.

He started to build his career from a young age. When he was five years old, he started to rap. He has his duo with Draft Pick which was signed to Priority Records at 12 yrs old.

The duo has played numerous gigs in New York City’s theatres, including Amateur Night at Apollo Theatre. Priority Records signed a contract with the duo. They stopped as perform as a Duo. They remain good friends.

Juelz Santana: Early, Profession life and Career

Juelz Santana was introduced to Cameron by his cousin. With Cam, he worked on various projects including “Double Up”, a track from Cam’s album SDE, Cam’s singles “Oh Boy” and “Come Home With Me.” He forms his group, The Diplomats along with Jim Jones, 40 Cal, Hell Rell, Jha-Jha, SAS, and Freekey Zeekey.

Juelz has his own creation of rhyming as he releases out his solo joining with Diplomats. He started his solo career by releasing his solo tape Final Destination in 2002. He releases his debut album From Me To U in 2003. The album was so hit and reached the 8th position on the Billboard 200 list.

After releasing a debut album, he recorded numerous singles including “There It Go”, “Oh Yes” and “Make It Work For Ya” for his second album What the Game’s Been Missing. In 2005, he was back again with this second album. The album is a hit and sold 141,000 copies within a few weeks. His albums and singles have reached the top rank in Billboard Hot 100.

On January 14, 2013, he released his third studio album God Will. He worked with various popular personalities including Wale, Jadakiss, Lloyd Banks, Yo Gotti, Lil Wayne, and Wiz Khalifa to make his third album.

Is Juelz Santana involved in any rumors and controversies?

He was engaged in some kind of legal issue in January 2011. The Bergen County Prosecutors Office checked him and as said by them, they found two loaded guns, 9mm handguns, boxes of bullets, and marijuana in his recording studio. The rapper was arrested by police on the spot.

He got arrested on the charge of possessing a firearm without a permit and collection of a dangerous substance. Santana missed his show on March 22, 2011, at Chris Brown record release after he was stopped by the police because they had a doubt that he may be carrying guns and drugs.

In March 2018, he was again found with a 38-caliber handgun and eight oxycodone pills. But he ran off and later surrender himself. Then in Dec, he was sent to prison for 27 months plus one year of supervised release.

However, in Aug 05, 2020, Juelz was released and had served only 17 months of his sentence.
