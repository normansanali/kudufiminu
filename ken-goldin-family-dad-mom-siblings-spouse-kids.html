Meet Ken Goldin Family

Ken Goldin is the son of Paul and Carole Goldin. He was married twice in his life but surprisingly, both of his wives seemingly have the same name; Jennifer.

Ken shared two older kids with his ex-wife, and two children with his second wife who are both under 10 years old.

Read about his parents, siblings, his spouses, and kids in this article below.

Ken Goldin Dad

Ken Goldin’s father is named Paul J Goldin. Paul passed away a while back. In fact, New York Times article reveals that he died in 1996 of a heart attack.

Paul was an adjunct professor of statistics at Drexel University in Philadelphia in 1986 when he became intrigued by the commercial possibilities of his son Ken’s baseball-card hobby. He created Score Board with Ken, who was 21 at the time, initially to sell and distribute baseball cards. The Cherry Hill, New Jersey-based publicly-traded corporation has since diversified into other collectibles, other sports, and general entertainment items.

Paul was 62 years old at the time of his death.

Ken Goldin Mom

Ken Goldin’s mother is Carole Goldin. At the time of her death, Carole Goldin was 77 years old when she passed away on 14 August 2016.

Carole’s mother was married twice. The other husband is named the late Julius Friedman.

How Many Siblings Does Ken Goldin Have?

Ken Goldin has one sibling. His sister is named Sharon Goldin. She was married to Roger who reportedly passed away. Sharon is a year older than Ken which makes her 59 years old.

Sharon appears to be living in Lauderhill, Florida.

Ken Goldin Spouse

Ken Goldin was married to his ex-wife Jennifer Goldin back in 1994. The wedding was announced on paper in October of that year. The couple is no longer married. He was married to his first wife after a proposal in Puerto Rico, but the marriage ended in divorce. Ken and his estranged ex-wife shared two children from their marriage.

Surprisingly, Ken’s second wife is also named Jennifer. In November 2021, on their date night, he posted a pic of his wife and captioned it, “Date night and yes I still have and wear “ the shirt”. With Jenn Goldin ( not on IG).”

Ken Goldin Kids

Ken and Jennifer Goldin are parents to two children; a son named Paul and a daughter named Carleigh. Paul was born in August 2013 which makes him 9 years old. On Paul’s 8th birthday, Ken surprised his son with a visit from his favorite player Tyrese Maxey.

Ken and Jennifer registered Carleigh’s birth in October 2017.

As stated earlier, Ken also has two children from a previous marriage. His daughter Laura is a prominent part of ‘King of Collectibles: The Goldin Touch’ season 1. Speaking during episode 4, Laura Goldin says that when her dad is boarding a private jet, she always has to go along with him.

In February 2022, Laura brought her mom to tailgate day.

Laura is working as Assistant Media Planner at Merkley + Partners. Other companies she worked for include Cashman & Associates, Bleecker Trading, Delta Delta Delta Sorority, and Goldin Auctions. Talking about her education, she attended Syracuse University – Maxwell School, Syracuse University – Martin J. Whitman School of Management, and Syracuse University College of Arts and Sciences.

In February 2023, Laura turned 22 years old.

Ken’s other daughter is named Lindsey Goldin. Lindsey celebrated her 26th birthday in July 2022. She is currently working as Content Acquisition Coordinator at The Orchard starting in 2021. Before that, she worked at companies such as WNRN, Community Dispute Settlement Center, WERS 88.9 FM.

Lindsey attended Emerson College. According to her Facebook, Lindsey lives in Brooklyn, New York. You can find Linsey on Instagram (@lind.zee).
