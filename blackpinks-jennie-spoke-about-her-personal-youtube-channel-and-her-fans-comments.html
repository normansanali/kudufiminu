BLACKPINKs Jennie Spoke About Her Personal YouTube Channel And Her Fans Comments

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Jennie of global K-pop phenomenon BLACKPINK recently welcomed fans and surprised them with some new stuff. The K-pop star shared a humorous video on her official YouTube account about her self-care regimen and everyday routines. While many admirers see Jennie as a role model and a celebrity, many were curious about how she maintains her glamorous lifestyle. Being a celebrity isn’t always easy, and Jennie shared some advice on how to keep good habits and ideas.

In her most recent video, Jennie also discussed her new YouTube account and said that she has been incredibly anxious to explore the comment area and read remarks left by fans about her or linked to her.

In her latest YouTube video, BLACKPINK’s Jennie reads heartfelt fan comments.

BLACKPINK Jennie’s most recent YouTube video has the singer discussing her beauty rituals for both day and night. She also shared some advice on daily morning routines that she enjoys doing after waking up. While wrapping up her self-care routine video, the Ice Cream singer noted that she has become more interested in the comment area since launching her personal YouTube account. Jennie’s coworkers encouraged her to instantly browse through the comments so she could randomly put her finger on a remark and read it aloud.

Jennie instantly agreed to the enjoyable endeavor after hearing the intriguing proposal. She stated:

“Oh! That is something I really want to do!”

Jennie then scrolled through the comments section of her last Hawaii video and came upon a sweet fan remark in English that perfectly summed up the BOOMBAYAH singer’s attitude. The remark was as follows:

“Her dress sense is stunning and sophisticated. She’s not simply an idol; she’s also a regular person who enjoys nice things, just like me. Jennie, I adore you!! I hope you had a good time. I can’t wait to see you in another wonderful vlog. Thank you very much for this one!!”

Jennie blushed as a result of the comforting words, and a huge grin came on the singer’s attractive face. She stated:

“What a lovely remark.”

BLACKPINK’s Jennie then decided to go on a mini-challenge by searching for fan comments in Korean, which she would love to read. According to the BLACKPINK rapper:

“I’ll attempt to read a Korean remark! I love discovering Korean comments! What exactly is that expression? Find one among many… Yes, it’s like looking for a needle in a haystack. It’s like a personal struggle for me.”

After discovering a message in Korean, Jennie read it aloud and said that it was a journal-like comment. A fan had remarked about Jennie’s recent vacation to Hawaii:

“Hawaii has breathtaking landscapes. I wish I could join you there. They claim Hawaii is the ideal vacation destination.”

Although the statement did not specifically mention or describe Jennie, the K-pop star agreed with the fan and said that Hawaii is a wonderful and unforgettable holiday destination.

Meanwhile, BLACKPINK’s Jennie discussed her newly launched platform channel, telling fans that she would work hard to produce more stuff for them to enjoy. She said that she will confer with her crew colleagues about developing more entertaining videos, such as Q&A sessions with the Blinks, and establishing a YouTube username for her viewers.
