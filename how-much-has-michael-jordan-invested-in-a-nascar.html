How much has Michael Jordan invested in a NASCAR?

August 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Michael Jordan, the legendary former basketball player and owner of the Charlotte Hornets, has made significant investments in the world of NASCAR, particularly through his ownership of the NASCAR team "23XI Racing." This article delves into the extensive involvement and investments of Michael Jordan in the NASCAR racing industry.

Background and Involvement in NASCAR

Michael Jordan's involvement in NASCAR stems from his lifelong passion for racing. Growing up in Wilmington, North Carolina, Jordan's father introduced him to Cup Series races at nearby tracks, fostering his love for the sport. Over the years, Jordan has expressed his deep appreciation for NASCAR, citing his admiration for racing icons such as Dale Earnhardt Sr. and Richard Petty.

In 2020, Michael Jordan made a significant move by partnering with NASCAR Cup Series driver Denny Hamlin to establish the 23XI Racing team. Jordan's investment of $150 million positioned him as the majority owner of the organization, while Hamlin became a minority owner. The team's name, "23XI Racing," pays homage to Jordan's iconic No. 23 jersey number and Hamlin's No. 11 for Joe Gibbs Racing, symbolizing their collaboration in the NASCAR realm.

23XI Racing Team

As of 2023, 23XI Racing features the No. 23, 45, and 67 Toyota teams, with notable drivers such as Bubba Wallace, Tyler Reddick, Travis Pastrana, and Kamui Kobayashi. This demonstrates Jordan's commitment to nurturing and supporting talent within the NASCAR circuit.

Post-Hornets Investment

Following the sale of his majority share in the Charlotte Hornets, Michael Jordan has demonstrated a heightened focus on his NASCAR team, 23XI Racing. Co-owner Denny Hamlin confirmed that Jordan has become more involved in the day-to-day operations of the racing team, attending more meetings and closely following the branding aspects of the organization. This increased involvement underscores Jordan's dedication to furthering the success of 23XI Racing in the NASCAR landscape.

Implications of Investment

Michael Jordan's foray into NASCAR not only exemplifies his personal passion for the sport but also highlights his broader interest in diversified investments. The creation of 23XI Racing and his subsequent deep involvement in the team following the Charlotte Hornets sale reflect Jordan's strategic approach to business and his commitment to making a lasting impact in the world of motorsports.

Michael Jordan's investments in NASCAR, particularly through the establishment of 23XI Racing, have solidified his presence in the motorsports industry. His substantial financial commitment, combined with his hands-on approach to team management, demonstrates his unwavering dedication to the success and growth of the NASCAR team. Jordan's transition into the world of motorsports further cements his legacy as a multifaceted entrepreneur and sports enthusiast, leaving an indelible mark on the NASCAR landscape.

As Michael Jordan continues to navigate the realms of business and sports, his investments in NASCAR stand as a testament to his enduring influence and wide-ranging impact beyond the basketball court.
