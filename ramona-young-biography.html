Who is Ramona Young?

Ramona Young is an American actress, writer, and producer. Ramona is famous for the role of Angelica in Blockers (Comedy) 2018, Mona Wu in DC’s Legends of Tomorrow (TV Series) from 2018 to 2020, and Eleanor Wong in Never Have I Ever (TV Series) 2020.

Furthermore, Young will be seen as Sweetie in Wendell and Wild (Animation Adventure) which is in Post-Production.

Ramona Young: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

Ramona was born on 8th August 1988 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Her full name is Ramona Abish Young. Talking about Young’s parent’s identities is still behind the curtains.

However, her father is a Psychologist and professor meanwhile her mother is in the Navy. Besides that, Young holds American nationality and belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity.

Besides, she is close to her grandparents as well. Her grandfather was a doctor and her grandmother sold clothes.

Moving towards her educational background, she graduated from California State University. Later, She joined acting at Playhouse West.

Ramona Young: Career

Ramona debuted as Eve in Specter (Short) in 2010. She played the role of Kevin’s Rude Fan in This Indie Thing (TV Series) in 2012. Besides that, in 2014 she was seen as Geeky Girl #2 in Super Fun Night (TV Series).

Moreover, Young was cast as Florence Ming in Reflections (Short) in 2015. In the following year, she played the role of Brit Jones in Subversion (Short). She played the role of Allison in The Real O’ Neals (TV Series) in 2017.

Furthermore, Ramona Young made her appearance as Marilyn in Bottomland (Short) in 2018. She was seen as Elenor Wong in Never Have I Ever (TV Series) in 2020. In addition, she will be seen as Rhonda in Uprising! (Short).
