Bella Hadid Enjoys the French Riviera Beach in a Pretty Pink Bikini

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Bella Hadid is having the dreamiest French Riviera holiday.

Just hours before making her grand return to the red carpet for the 77th Cannes Film Festival, the supermodel was spotted lounging on the beach Sunday at the luxurious Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc hotel in Antibes, France. The glamorous Mediterranean resort town lies between Cannes and Nice on the Côte d’Azur.

The Orebella founder enjoyed a morning in the ocean in a frilly baby-pink and white triangle bikini by Australian label With Jéan. The romantic two-piece is called the Juni, and Hadid’s is in the color rosewater. Each piece of the suit features an abstract cotton candy–esque print made even sweeter by the pretty pink stitching along the sides and the stringy ties of the halter top and bottoms. Crafted from recycled polyester, spandex, and sustainable yarn, the swimwear set is available in sizes XXS–XL and is currently on sale for $100.

Splash News

Hadid wore the bikini simply with elongated silver-frame shades and some gold bangles. She was seen holding a small digital camera while walking on some rocks by the water.

Related Stories

After leaving the beach, Hadid made a spectacular arrival in Cannes in a slinky brown minidress and pointy pink kitten heels.

On Monday, she exuded glamour when she walked the red carpet at the Palais des Festivales in a latte brown mesh naked dress, courtesy of Saint Laurent, which featured intricate ruching from her torso to her upper thighs. She paired it with glossy peep-toe heels in a chocolate brown and glittering Chopard diamond earrings.

And today, Hadid again returned to the carpet in a golden halterneck minidress that made her glow like the sun. She finished her vibrant look with cream kitten heels, amber-tinted rectangular sunglasses, and curvy diamond earrings.
